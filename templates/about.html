How It Works

1

Choose Your Service

Thank you for choosing Senmaa Logistic Solutions! We are delighted to offer you a wide range of reliable and efficient logistics services to meet your transportation and supply chain needs. Please explore our services below and select the one that best suits your requirements:

  • Air Freight: Swift and reliable air freight services for time-sensitive and global shipments.
  • Ocean Freight: Cost-effective sea transportation for larger and heavier cargo across international waters.
  • Warehousing: Secure and flexible storage options to accommodate your inventory needs.
  • Storage: Safe and secure storage solutions for short-term and long-term requirements.
  • Ground Shipping: Nationwide coverage with timely and versatile ground shipping solutions for domestic shipments.
2

Select Your Payment

Thank you for choosing Senmaa Logistic Solutions for your logistics needs. We offer various secure and convenient payment options to make your transaction process smooth and hassle-free. Please select your preferred payment method from the following options:

  • Credit/Debit Card: Pay securely using your Visa, MasterCard, American Express, or Discover credit/debit card. We use encrypted payment gateways to safeguard your financial information.
  • Bank Transfer: Transfer the payment directly from your bank account to ours. Our bank details will be provided upon selecting this option.
  • PayPal: Use your PayPal account to make a secure payment. PayPal offers a trusted and widely used online payment platform.
  • Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT): Make an electronic transfer of funds directly to our account. Our EFT details will be provided upon selecting this option.
  • Invoice: If you have a credit account with us, you can choose to pay using the invoice option. Our finance team will issue an invoice for your payment.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD): For select services and locations, you can choose to pay in cash upon delivery of your shipment. Please note that COD may not be available for all services.
3
